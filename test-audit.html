AUDIT - World Health Organization, 1989 (revised 2001)

The AUDIT is the gold standard alcohol screening tool developed by the World Health Organization. Used in over 50 countries, it identifies hazardous drinking, harmful use, and possible alcohol dependence. In high-pressure entertainment environments, alcohol is often used as a coping mechanism for stress, loneliness, and performance anxiety.

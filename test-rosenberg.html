Rosenberg Self-Esteem Scale

RSES - Morris Rosenberg, 1965

The Rosenberg Self-Esteem Scale is the most widely used measure of global self-esteem worldwide. It evaluates your overall sense of self-worth and personal value. In the entertainment industry, self-esteem is constantly tested by public scrutiny, comparison culture, rejection, and the pressure to maintain a public image that may differ greatly from one's true self.

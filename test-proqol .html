ProQOL-5 - Stamm, 2010. Compassion Fatigue and Satisfaction

The ProQOL measures the positive and negative aspects of caring for others in professional roles. Unlike other burnout tools, it captures both what drains you and what sustains you. Particularly relevant for psychologists, managers, coordinators, and anyone in the creative industry who holds ongoing responsibility for other people's well-being.

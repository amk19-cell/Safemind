Psychological Crisis Resources

If you are in danger right now,
please call emergency services.

Creaven is an online platform. We cannot replace physical emergency services. If someone is in immediate physical danger, call your country's emergency number first. We are here for what comes after.

⚠️
Important: Creaven is an online psychological well-being service
We provide prevention, support, and consultations through our platform. In cases of acute crisis involving a risk to life such as a suicide attempt, self-harm, or a psychiatric emergency requiring physical intervention, please contact your local emergency services immediately. Creaven psychologists can support you before and after a crisis, but physical emergencies require physical responders.
💚
What Creaven can do for you
Creaven is available for crisis consultations when you are in psychological distress but not in immediate physical danger. If you feel overwhelmed, anxious, on the edge, or simply need to talk to someone right now, book an emergency consultation. We will connect you with a psychologist as quickly as possible. We are one call away.

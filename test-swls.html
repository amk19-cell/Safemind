Satisfaction With Life Scale

SWLS - Diener, Emmons, Larsen and Griffin, 1985

The Satisfaction With Life Scale is the most widely used measure of cognitive well-being and life satisfaction. It assesses your overall evaluation of your life as a whole. In the creative industry, life satisfaction is often disrupted by the gap between public success and private fulfillment, the unpredictability of career trajectories, and the sacrifice of personal milestones for professional goals.

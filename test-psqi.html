Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index

PSQI - Buysse, Reynolds, Monk, Berman and Kupfer, 1989

The Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index measures sleep quality and disturbances over the past month across 7 components. In the entertainment industry, irregular schedules, late-night rehearsals, international tours, and constant performance pressure make sleep disorders one of the most prevalent yet least addressed health issues.

Sleep is the foundation of everything.

Poor sleep affects mood, cognitive performance, physical health, and emotional regulation. Our team can help you understand the root causes of your sleep difficulties and develop practical strategies tailored to your schedule and lifestyle.

Important notice: The PSQI is a validated clinical instrument for assessing sleep quality. It is not a diagnosis of a sleep disorder. A score above 5 suggests clinically significant sleep problems that warrant professional evaluation.

What We Offer

Four pillars of care

Every service is delivered 100% remotely and tailored to your organization's culture and specific needs.

01

Prevention & Psychoeducation

Building psychological literacy and awareness across the entire organization - from mental health basics to addiction, harassment, and identity.

Workshops Webinars Resources
02

Listening & Individual Support

Confidential individual sessions providing a safe space to be heard, assessed, and guided - with referral to clinical professionals when needed.

1-on-1 Sessions Follow-up Referral
03

Team & Management Training

Equipping managers and team leaders with tools to recognize distress signals, respond appropriately, and build a culture of psychological safety.

Leadership EQ Training Protocols
04

Protocols & Organizational Safety

Designing robust systems - from minor protection to crisis management and suicide prevention procedures - that protect employees at the structural level.

Risk Audit Policy Design Crisis Plans
Our People

Everyone matters here

If you work for the organization, you are included. No role too small, no position too junior. From CEO to intern - Safe Mind is for all of you.

🎤

Artists & Performers

Singers, dancers, actors, musicians. Those who give everything on stage and carry what no one sees when the lights go off.

🎬

Production & Technical

Camera crews, sound engineers, lighting teams, stage builders. The hands that make the show exist, invisible in the applause.

📋

Management & Coordination

Managers, coordinators, schedulers. The people who absorb every pressure so others can perform, with no one asking how they are.

👥

Human Resources

HR teams, recruiters, talent managers. They hold the human side of organizations together. They too carry the weight of others.

✍️

Creative & Media

Designers, photographers, content creators, social media teams. Building culture daily, often alone, often without recognition.

🛠️

Support & Operations

Drivers, catering staff, logistics, administration. Without them nothing moves. They are the foundation no one photographs.

🌱

Interns & Early Careers

Those just starting, carrying the most while having the least protection. Their passion deserves the same care as anyone else.

🔒

Security & Safety

Security staff, bodyguards, event safety officers. They protect everyone else. Who protects them?

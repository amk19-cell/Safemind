Consultations

A safe space to speak freely.

Every Safe Mind consultation is confidential, conducted remotely, and available in your language. Audio or video. No travel, no waiting room, no stigma.

How it works
From booking to your first session
1
Book your session
Choose your consultation type and select a time. You receive a confirmation email with your session code.
2
Receive your code
A unique session code is sent to your email 30 minutes before the session. Your psychologist has the matching code.
3
Join your session
Enter your code below and choose audio or video mode. The connection is direct, encrypted, and private.
4
Speak in your language
Enable real-time voice translation. You speak in your language, your psychologist hears in theirs. Instantly.

You speak. We translate.
In real time.
Safe Mind integrates real-time voice translation powered by Azure Speech Translation. Your psychologist speaks French. You hear Korean. Instantly. No interpreter, no delay, no misunderstanding. The conversation flows naturally in both directions.
Supported languages
English Francais 한국어 日本語 中文 ไทย Deutsch Espanol

PHQ-9 - Kroenke, Spitzer & Williams, 2001 | Depression Screening

The PHQ-9 is the world's most widely used depression screening tool, adopted by clinicians, hospitals, and organizations across over 80 countries. It measures the nine core symptoms of major depression as defined by the DSM-5. In entertainment and high-performance industries, depression often goes unrecognized - the PHQ-9 is a first step toward understanding what you may be experiencing.
